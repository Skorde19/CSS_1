Grilled Cheese Sandwiches with Bacon and Tomato

Ingredients:

  • Bacon
  • Tomato slices
  • Cheddar cheese
  • Butter
  • Mayonnaise
  • Bread slices

Instruction :

1.Cook backon until crispy.

2.Spread butter on one side of each bread slice.

3.Place one slice of bread,butter side down, in a skillet over medium heat.

4.Layer cheese, bacon, tomato slices, and more cheese on top of the bread slice in the skillet.

5. Top with the reamaining bread slice,butter side up.

6.Cook until the bread is goladen brown and the cheese is melted ,fliping half way through.

7.Serve hot and enjoy!
